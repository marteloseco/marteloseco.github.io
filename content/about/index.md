## From Anvil to API

I spent the first part of my career as a blacksmith - yes, an actual blacksmith. Worked with metal, fire, and hammers for years. But I realized the future wasn't in forging horseshoes, so I taught myself to code and made the switch to software development.

Turns out the skills transfer pretty well. Both require shaping raw materials into something useful, fixing things when they break, and knowing when to strike while the iron is hot (or in this case, when to ship while the idea is fresh).

## What I'm Building

I'm a full-stack developer focused on shipping fast and iterating based on real user feedback. My toolkit includes:

- Web development (React, Node.js, Python)

## My Philosophy

Move fast. Launch early. Learn from failures. The best way to validate an idea is to get it in front of users, not to perfect it in private.

I've had my share of projects that didn't work out, but each one taught me something valuable. That's the game.
